As a group, you need to use the information provided to try to predict how the price of each food type will change throughout the coming year. Once you have done this, you should use your budget to purchase the foods you believe will have the greatest increase in price over the coming year. (If you believe that all 3 food types will decrease in price, then you would purchase the foods whose price you believe will decrease the least.)

Example: In the event that you were confident the price of Sugar would rise more than the price of both Wheat and Coffee. Then the best method would be invest all your available budget in Sugar.
