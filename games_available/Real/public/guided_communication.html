Group Chat


Instructions: Use the chat box to exchange information you have been given with the other members of your group. To send a message:
1. Click on one of the buttons on the left to display a piece of information and press the "message" button next to it's Topic.
2. Use the Belief drop down menus to indicate what you think this piece of information is suggesting.
3. Provide a brief explanation of your thinking in the Justification box.
4. Press the Send button.
Note: You must send at least 1 piece of information.
